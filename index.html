The "Goldilocks Zone" of Pressure

Myofascial release requires specific sustained pressure. Too little is useless; too much causes defensive muscle guarding. The SmartSphere ensures users stay in the Therapeutic Window.

Guided vs. Unguided

Studies show that visual biofeedback improves motor learning by 35% compared to verbal instruction alone. The app provides this visual link.

Safety First

Sensors detect bony prominences (sudden spike in resistance) and warn the user to shift position, preventing bruising.

Feasibility Verdict: HIGH
